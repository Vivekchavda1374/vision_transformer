About Vision Transformer

Transformer Architecture

Uses the powerful Transformer architecture, originally designed for NLP, adapted for computer vision tasks.

High Accuracy

Achieves state-of-the-art performance on image classification benchmarks with proper training data.

Patch-based Processing

Divides images into patches and processes them as sequences, enabling global context understanding.

Attention Mechanism

Uses self-attention to focus on relevant parts of the image for better classification accuracy.
